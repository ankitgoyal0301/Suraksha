  • ≡   A rape in India every 15 minutes.

    In India, a rape is reported every 15 minutes, according to recently released official government crime data. The National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) data from 2018 has stark revelations about crime in the country. In almost 94 per cent of the cases, the offenders were known to the victims - family members, friends, live-in partners, employers or others, the data showed.

  • ≡   99% of voilence faced by women go unnoticed.

    According to, National Family Health Survey (NFHS) average Indian woman is 17 times more likely to face sexual violence from her husband than from others. Only 3.5% of victims of violence reported seeking the help of police. In states such as Bihar, Uttar Pradesh and Jharkhand, less than 0.5% of incidents of violence against women were reported. Tamil Nadu and Karnataka also saw very low reporting of such crimes despite a higher female literacy rate.
